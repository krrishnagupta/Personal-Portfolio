Hello! I’m Krishna Kumar Gupta, a passionate and driven 4th-year Computer Science student with a strong focus on full-stack web development, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. My goal is to create innovative solutions by merging creativity with technology.

I’ve had the privilege of interning as a front-end developer at Priority Technologies Pvt Ltd, where I gained practical experience in building responsive and scalable web applications. I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C++, and Python, along with frameworks like React, Express.js, Node.js, MongoDB, and Bootstrap. I am a strong believer in working hard with smartness, giving my best in any task, and continuously pushing myself toward growth. My hobbies, like playing chess and watching anime, keep me balanced and inspired.

I’m always open to new collaborations, exciting projects, and opportunities to learn and grow. Feel free to connect if you’d like to work together or discuss technology!
